we are making a Text adventure where the user chooses between 3 different options for each situation to try and get to the end of the story

End users

Our end users are students at western spring college (WSC). We know a lot of things about students at WSC such as, we know a lot of them are going to be teenagers who like fiction and fantasy stories, they are probably going to be playing this game is class since they are most likely bored and don't want to only do school work meaning our story would have to be short so they can do their work and finish the game.

Requirements

our game must be appealing to the students at WSC we have adressed this by

  1. detail: our story needs to be short but with detail to appeal meaning it has to be discreptive every action should be described.
  2. Fun: like all games ours has to be fun meaning our story should be entertaining and have lots of options to keep the game new and fresh
  3. understandable: our game needs to be simple but with options and not messy so the user knows whats happening and what their options are in every situation
  4. story: our game must have a short story for people to complete during school but also must be entertaining to keep people playing
